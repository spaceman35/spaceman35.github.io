DuckDuckGo
Ditch google and use DuckDuckGo for truly private search. Google has its uses, but I use DDG when possible.

Firefox Multi-Account Containers
Firefox Multi-Account Containers lets you keep parts of your online life separated into color-coded tabs that preserve your privacy. Cookies are separated by container, allowing you to use the web with multiple identities or accounts simultaneously.

Have I Been Pwned?
Check if you have an account that has been compromised in a data breach.

Data Detox Kit
Everyday steps you can take to control your digital privacy, security, and wellbeing in ways that feel right to you.

Waterfox
Respin of FireFox with some tweaks for enhanced privacy and security.
